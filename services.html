Training and Behavioural Solutions

Whether you're dealing with puppyhood antics or addressing specific behavioural issues, our certified trainers can help you establish a strong bond with your pet and teach them essential obedience commands. Our training services include:

  • Basic obedience training to teach your pet commands like sit, stay, come, and heel
  • Puppy socialization to help your young pet learn appropriate interactions with people and other dog
  • Behavioural modification to address issues like excessive barking, separation anxiety, and leash pulling
  • Training for specific needs such as teaching service dogs their tasks or helping pets overcome fear of thunder

Veterinary Care

For your pet's ongoing health and well-being, Pet Palace offers a range of veterinary services, including:

  • Wellness exams to check your pet's overall health and detect any potential issues early on
  • Vaccinations to protect your pet from preventable diseases
  • Parasite prevention to keep your pet free from fleas, ticks, and worms
  • Minor illness and injury treatment for common ailments and accidents
  • Dental care for routine cleaning, extractions, and other oral health needs

Daycare and Playtime

Our daycare facilities are designed with your pet's safety and comfort in mind. We have spacious indoor and outdoor play areas, supervised by our experienced and attentive staff, ensuring your pet can have fun and socialize without worry.. Our daycare services include:

  • Full Day Care: Keep your pet engaged and entertained from morning to evening with our full-day daycare program.
